Why Is Starting So Hard?

Some people do not struggle to do the work. They struggle to start it. You know exactly what to do, it matters to you, and you still do not begin. Then the not-starting becomes its own weight.

This assessment looks at four different reasons starting stalls, and shows you which one is actually yours. About 4 minutes. You get a personalised read at the end. There are no right answers, and it is not about willpower.
